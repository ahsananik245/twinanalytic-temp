Feasibility & Cost Estimation

AI-Driven Cost Sizing

Our Feasibility & Cost Estimation service links structural dimensions directly to material cost databases (5D BIM). Whenever a design modification is made inside the software, our automated spreadsheets extract the new concrete volume or steel rebar weight, updating quantity takeoffs instantly. This real-time budgeting allows developers to test options without design lag.

To support green building initiatives, we also run structural lifecycle analysis (LCA). By testing carbon footprint indexes of different steel and concrete grades, we recommend material mixtures that keep structures highly cost-effective and carbon-efficient.

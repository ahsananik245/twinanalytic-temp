Privacy Policy

GDPR Compliance Statement

At TwinAnalytic, we take your privacy and data security seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines our data processing methods in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable European data protection guidelines.

1. Data Controller Information

The entity responsible for processing your personal data (Data Controller) is:

TwinAnalytic GmbH
Level 48, Onyx Tower
Frankfurt am Main, Germany
Email: privacy@twinanalytic.com

2. Types of Data Collected

We only collect data that is voluntarily supplied by you or generated dynamically during your use of our structural calculator suite. This includes:

  • Contact Form Details: Full Name, Business Email Address, Phone Number, and Project Summary details submitted when requesting a quote or coordination consultation.
  • Calculator Outputs: Calculated concrete volumes, steel reinforcement weights, and D/C ratios calculated using our interactive calculators (stored in your browser local storage).

3. Purpose and Legal Basis of Processing

We process your personal information under the following legal bases of GDPR:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): When you explicitly submit your details through our contact form.
  • Contractual Obligations (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): To coordinate project specifications and transmit design proposal quotes.
  • Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): To verify the correct engineering calculations and monitor the stability of our web-app.

4. Data Retention Policy

Personal data collected via our contact form is retained in our secure lead logs for a period of 12 months following submission, after which it is automatically anonymized or deleted, unless a longer retention period is required by German commercial laws or active contract negotiations.

5. Your GDPR Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you possess the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR): The right to obtain confirmation and copy of your stored data.
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR): The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete details.
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR): The right to be forgotten and have your details deleted from our servers.
  • Right to Restriction (Art. 18 GDPR): The right to restrict data processing.
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR): The right to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR): The right to object to the processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our data protection officer at privacy@twinanalytic.com.

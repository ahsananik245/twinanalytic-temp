Quality Auditing & Mixed Reality

Construction Supervision

As-Built vs. As-Designed Audits

Our construction supervision division connects digital models directly to physical execution. Using mixed-reality headsets (HoloLens 2), site inspectors overlay 3D blueprints directly onto constructed walls, structural rebars, and piping layouts. This allows for immediate detection of deviation coordinates, preventing expensive assembly conflicts before systems are enclosed.

We track QA parameters continuously, logging concrete core compression test outcomes, layout tolerances, and mechanical checklists. Every audit is synced with our cloud platform, keeping developers informed of execution compliance metrics.

Our Execution Methodology

01

HoloLens AR Model Calibration

Establishing spatial control points on-site and matching physical building columns with coordinate markers inside the AR viewport.

02

Structural Rebar Verification

Overlaying high-fidelity rebar schematics directly onto formworks prior to casting, auditing spacing limits and steel sizing.

03

Concrete Cylinder Compression Logs

Taking on-site sample cylinders and tracking concrete compression curves to verify M25/M30/M40 specification compliance.

04

Cloud Deviation Matrix Sync

Mapping deviation points found in the field directly back to the cloud model registry, creating immediate corrective task logs.

"Using Trimble Connect AR on the HoloLens 2, the construction supervision crew at the Vertex Tower isolated 42 alignment issues before concrete pouring, avoiding costly post-pour rework."

Core Workflows & Deliverables

  • Augmented Reality Overlays: In-field visual verification of structure alignments using HoloLens.
  • Concrete Compression Tracking: Digitally logging cylinder test curves for M25/M30 concrete zones.
  • Quality Compliance Audits: Generating checklists mapping field state to design codes.
  • Defect Log Mapping: Pinpointing location data of defects directly onto the master model.
